Equal worth, equal justice

Washington Civil and Disability Advocates is guided by the simple but powerful proposition that all lives have equal dignity and worth, and each are uniquely made in the image of God. As a result, this organization is committed to providing low or no cost legal services to individuals in need of protection of their civil rights.
